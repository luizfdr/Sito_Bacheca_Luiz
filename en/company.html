The Chevalier Estate

Our Roots

Tenuta Chevalier is a unique and artisanal reality

Located in Valpolicella Classica, in the municipalities of Marano and Negrar. We own 3 hectares of our own vineyards, all organic since 2009, and others that we are converting. Through the knowledge of Dr. Paolo Zardini, agronomist and oenologist, owner of Tenuta Chevalier.

"Use your head and knowledge is the business rule!"

A decisive step imposed by the property is that the company is moving towards a zero impact management system, sinking its vision into the study area of biodynamic and ecological cultivation.

Philosophy

Organic & Biodynamic

Our vineyards at 350 meters altitude with east-west exposure. Clayey-silty, calcareous, volcanic, and alluvial soils.

Organic Since 2009

No chemicals - only Copper and Sulfur using the 1885 formula introduced by Pierre-Marie Alexis Millardet. Guyot and double pergola training systems.

Zero Impact

Moving towards biodynamic and ecological cultivation. Grass-covered vineyards worked seasonally. The future of sustainable winemaking.

Visionary

Dr. Paolo Zardini

Agronomist and oenologist, owner of Tenuta Chevalier. His knowledge and passion drive our commitment to quality, tradition, and innovation in Valpolicella Classica.

Chevalier Legacy

Chevalier is a French name, by virtue of the fact that the first owner was indeed a French winemaker lady. Located in one of the noblest areas of the Classica zone.
