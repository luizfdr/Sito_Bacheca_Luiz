Wines

From our organic vineyards in Valpolicella Classica

DOCG

Amarone della Valpolicella Classico

Vineyards in Negrar di Valpolicella and Marano di Valpolicella. 350m altitude, east-west exposure. Clayey-silty soils, Guyot and double pergola training. Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella blend with rigorous grape selection.

AROMA

Primarily fruity scents: cherry, black cherry, currant, blackberry, plum. Dried fruit, jam. Floral: rose, violet. Spicy: cinnamon, licorice, nutmeg. Barrel aging adds vanilla, toasted, coffee, cocoa, chocolate, pipe tobacco. Tertiary: leather, undergrowth, mint, eucalyptus.

PAIRING

Tasty meats, game, roasts, braised meats. Aged cheeses, spicy condiments. Verona classics: horse stew, donkey or duck ragù.

DOC

Valpolicella Classico DOC

The vineyards of the Chevalier estate are located in the Municipality of Negrar di Valpolicella and Marano di Valpolicella. Late harvest technique (October) for maximum freshness and fragrance.

CHARACTER

Full-bodied red wine, very lean, fresh, fragrant, dry, and pleasant.

PAIRING

Excellent aperitif (15-16°C). Complex dishes: fish, shellfish, pad thai, tuna tataki. Hearty dishes: bigoli with duck, pizza bianca, torta fritta, paella, vitello tonnato, chicken curry, pork ribs (18-20°C).

DOC Superiore

Valpolicella Classico Superiore

Selected grapes from our finest plots. Ruby red color with garnet notes, ethereal vanilla scent, refined, harmonious, dry and velvety flavor.

AROMA

Intense and persistent with cherry, plum, violet, and black pepper notes.

PAIRING

Traditional Veronese dishes: lasagna with rabbit, tortelloni with Monte Veronese, cured meats.

IGT Corvina 100%

Rocchetto

100% Corvina from 30-year-old vineyard in Negrar hills (350m). Late harvest with appassimento on the vine - ancient Raetian technique concentrating flavors.

AROMA

Ruby with garnet highlights. Port-like, ripe cherry, raspberry, blackberry, undergrowth, dark chocolate, leather, cinnamon, vanilla, licorice, coffee, oriental spices.

PAIRING

Chicken curry, ribs, lasagna, truffle risotto, pulled pork, empanadas, game, spicy barbecue, aged cheeses, meditative tasting.

IGT

Passito Bianco "Pensiero"

Deep golden yellow with orange highlights. Grapes dried 4-5 months losing 40% weight. Vineyards 270-320m altitude, east-west exposure.

AROMA

Intense, delicately fruity with honey hints. Smooth, velvety flavor.

PAIRING

Sweet dessert wine with dry pastries and fresh Veronese cheeses. Serve 12-14°C. Ages 30-40+ years.
