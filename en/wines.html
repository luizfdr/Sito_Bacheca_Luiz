DOCG Red

Amarone della Valpolicella Classico

The crown jewel of Valpolicella. Made from Corvina, Corvinone, and Rondinella grapes, meticulously selected and dried to concentrate their essence.

🌿 The Vineyards

The vineyards are located in the Municipality of Negrar di Valpolicella and Marano di Valpolicella at approximately 350 meters above sea level. The training system is Guyot and double pergola.

🍷 The Wine

Primarily fruity scents: cherry, black cherry, currant, blackberry, plum. Dried fruit and in alcohol, jam. Floral scents: rose, violet. With aging, spicy notes emerge: cinnamon, licorice, nutmeg, cloves. Barrel aging adds vanilla, toasted, coffee, cocoa, chocolate and pipe tobacco notes.

🍴 Pairing

Excellent with tasty meats, game, roasts and braised meats. Very good with aged cheeses and spicy condiments. In Verona it is paired with horse stew, donkey or duck ragù, boiled meat.

DOC Red

Valpolicella Classico

A full-bodied red wine, very lean, fresh, fragrant, dry, and pleasant. In recent years, Tenuta Chevalier has adopted the late harvest technique for this wine.

🌿 The Vineyards

The vineyards are located in Negrar and Marano di Valpolicella at 350 meters altitude with East-West exposure. Various soil types: clayey-silty, calcareous, volcanic or alluvial.

🍷 The Wine

A full-bodied red wine, very lean, fresh, fragrant, dry, and pleasant. The late harvest technique (October) brings additional concentration and depth to this classic expression.

🍴 Pairing

Excellent as an aperitif at 15-16°C. Pairs well with fish, shellfish, pad thai, tuna tataki. At 18-20°C, enjoy with bigoli with duck, pizza bianca, paella, vitello tonnato, chicken curry, pork ribs, and baked lasagna.

DOC Red Superiore

Valpolicella Classico Superiore

Coming from selected grapes, the "Superiore" type has a characteristic ruby color and garnet notes, with a refined, harmonious, dry and velvety flavor.

🌿 The Vineyards

The same prestigious vineyards in Negrar and Marano di Valpolicella, with grapes selected more rigorously for this superior designation.

🍷 The Wine

Ruby red with garnet notes. Intense and persistent aroma with fruity notes of cherry and plum, floral notes of violets and slightly spicy notes of black pepper. Fresh, fruity flavor with elegant balance of structure and body.

🍴 Pairing

An ideal match for traditional Veronese dishes, such as lasagna with rabbit and tortelloni with Monte Veronese. Also excellent with cured meats!

IGT Rosso Verona Corvina in Purezza

Rocchetto

100% Corvina from a 30-year-old vineyard at peak maturity. An intriguing wine made from late-harvested grapes dried on the vine, an ancient technique to concentrate flavors, aromas, and sugars.

🌿 The Vineyards

100% Corvina grapes from a 30-year-old vineyard in the hilly part of Negrar della Valpolicella, approximately 350 meters above sea level. Late harvest (November/December) with grapes dried on the vine.

🍷 The Wine

Beautiful ruby color with garnet highlights. Complex aromas of Port wine, ripe cherry, raspberry, blackberry, undergrowth, dark chocolate, and leather. Spicy notes of cinnamon and vanilla. Warm, full-bodied, and magnificently structured.

🍴 Pairing

Classic meat dishes, chicken curry, ribs, baked lasagna, truffle risotto, game, spicy barbecues, fillet Wellington, aged cheeses. A wine that also finds its place in meditative tasting.

IGT White Passito Sweet

Passito Bianco "Pensiero"

A deep golden yellow wine with orange highlights; an intense and delicately fruity aroma with hints of honey; a smooth and velvety flavor. Truly a wine for contemplation.

🌿 The Vineyards

After manual harvesting, the grapes are dried for approximately 4-5 months in small wooden crates, during which time they lose 40% of their weight. Vineyards located between 270 and 320 meters above sea level, with east-west exposure.

🍷 The Wine

Deep golden yellow with orange highlights. Intense and delicately fruity aroma with hints of honey. Smooth and velvety flavor. These wines have a similar background to the wines produced in the Sauternes and Barsac areas, considered the best sweet wines in the world.

🍴 Pairing

This sweet dessert wine pairs well with dry pastries and certain fresh Veronese cheeses. Also recommended for aging outside the vintage of production. Serve at 12-14°C. Shelf life 30-40 years and beyond.
