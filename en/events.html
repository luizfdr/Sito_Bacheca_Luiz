Guided Experiences

Vineyard Tours & Tastings

Available by appointment. Immerse yourself in our organic vineyards and historic cellars

Vineyard Tour

Explore our 350m altitude vineyards in Negrar and Marano. Learn about organic practices, Guyot pergola systems, and terroir diversity: clay-silt, volcanic, alluvial soils.

Duration

90 minutes • Max 8 guests

Wine Tasting

Taste our portfolio: Amarone DOCG, Valpolicella Superiore, Rocchetto IGT, Passito "Pensiero". Guided by our oenologist through aromas, structure, and pairings.

Selections

4-6 wines • Cheese pairings
