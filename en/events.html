The experience

Guided Tastings

Experience an unforgettable journey through the flavors and aromas of Valpolicella. Our guided tastings offer a deep dive into the world of Tenuta Chevalier wines, led by knowledgeable hosts who share the stories behind each bottle.

From the powerful complexity of our Amarone della Valpolicella Classico to the delicate sweetness of our Passito Bianco "Pensiero," each tasting is designed to awaken your senses and deepen your appreciation for artisanal winemaking.

Tastings include:

  • guided tasting of 4-5 wines
  • Tour of the vineyards and cellar
  • Explanation of the winemaking process
  • Local food pairings (upon request)

Vineyard Tours

Walk through our organic vineyards in the municipalities of Negrar and Marano di Valpolicella. Learn about our sustainable cultivation methods, the unique terroir of Valpolicella Classica, and the grape varieties that define our wines.

Our tours offer an authentic behind-the-scenes look at the care and dedication that goes into every bottle. See the Guyot and double pergola training systems, understand our biodynamic approach, and feel the connection between the land and the wine.

Available year-round, with special experiences during:

  • Spring: Bud break and flowering
  • Summer: Veraison and canopy management
  • Autumn: Harvest season (September-October)
  • Winter: Pruning and cellar aging
