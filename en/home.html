Our Estate

The Heart of Valpolicella

Tenuta Chevalier is a unique and artisanal reality located in the Valpolicella Classica, in the municipalities of Marano and Negrar. We own 3 hectares of our own vineyards, all organic since 2009.

Through the knowledge of Dr. Paolo Zardini, agronomist and oenologist, owner of Tenuta Chevalier, the company is moving towards a zero impact management system, sinking its vision into the study area of biodynamic and ecological cultivation.
